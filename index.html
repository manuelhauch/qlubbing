About

Going out is about having fun with your friends in any kind of bar or club. To often we find ourselves arguing with our friens upon tonights club choice about which. In most of the cases this happens because we might not know if the clubs in question have a cool event going on tonight, if the club is actually worth going to or if the club is going to be either to crowded or too full any you missed your timeframe of making it in in time. Well that doesn't sound that much fun...

We, a small but ambitious group of students, are aiming to change the nightlife experience for the better. We are working on providing our users with information about events from clubs, bars and local student pubs. Furthermore our goal is to provide information about queuing times and the number of people at any kind of venue, all in real time. Yes you read that correctly. The information shown in our app indicates how crowded a club is! Perfect if you are at a preparty trying to decide which party you want to go to.

Currently, we are able to provide information about the events happening in Umea and are working hard on delivering our other core features such as the queuing and crowd size part. If you would like to be among the first couple of thousand users you might want to add your email adress to the subscribe bar dispalyed on the bottom of your screen.

Note: This app can only be downloaded by receiving an invitation. If you would like to use this service type in your email adress in the subscribe field and hit send! We'll send you an invite as soon as we can.
