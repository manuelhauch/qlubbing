E-Pub



Tentafest

Facebook Interested people:

Hello everyone! It's finally time for this months exam party at our beloved E-pub. As usual we open at 9pm so be sure to get there in time to ensure that you'll get in so that you can enjoy the evening to the fullest ;)

Entrance fee: 30SEK
Time: 9pm-2am
Do not forget your ID and student union card!

(We also want to encourage you all to leave your alcohol at the pre-party, alcohol in the queue is not allowed.)












Origo



Funky Fred at Karhuset Origo

Facebook Interested people:

Har CSN landat poa kontot och du laenner dig rik som en Ingenjoer? Har du precis haft tenta och vill dansa bort tentastressen?

Doa aer det dags att styra dina steg mot mittpunkten, allas voart Koarhus, foer en fantastisk kvaell i sann studentanda!

Funky Fred blandar loatarna du kan texten poa tillsammans med det baesta inom EDM. Vi river av allt ifroan gamla discodaengor froan -73 till de senaste releaserna.

Kom till Koarhuset Origo, du blir inte besviken!)
