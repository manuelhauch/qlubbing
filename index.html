About

Going out should be about having fun with your friends inside a nightclub or bar, and not standing in queues or arguing with your friends about tonights club choice.

We, a small but ambitious group of students, are aiming to change the nightlife experience for the better. We are working on an app which provides our users with information about queuing times and the number of people at local night clubs, all in real time.
