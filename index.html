Origo



Club X 2018

17th February, 21:00-02:00




Facebook event description:

Origo proudly presents this years edition of Club X!

Club X is an event held once each spring semester and focuses on electronic music and extreme sound and light. For one night Origo has rebuilt into one big dance floor and several of our DJ’s will be playing during the evening.

The recommended dress code is black and white since we will have more custom lighting in the room for you to enjoy. Lineup will be released later!

Everyone that attends the facebook event and is paying the entry fee before 23:30 is guaranteed 20 SEK discount on the entry fee.

Prices for the evening is:

FREE for staff level 2
20 SEK for staff level 1
40 SEK for NTK-members
65 SEK for students
85 SEK for guests


Hope to see you there!
